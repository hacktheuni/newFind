1.0 Master:

  • Here there are 3 sub tabs available, which can be used for creating Master. Data saved in these Master Records can be used for repetitive uses.

1.1 Group:

  • This tab helps you to Create Group Master.
  • If you have certain group of clients, you can segregate them in different groups.
  • There is a default Group named “NONE”.

1.2 Company:

  • This tab helps you to create list of Company/ LLP/ Partnership Firms/ Proprietorship Firms.
  • If the company belongs to any specific group then you can tag that group from Master.
  • In case entity doesn’t have any specific group then you can tag NONE as entity group.
  • System will not allow saving Same Company Name tagged with Same Group.
  • In case same entity name belongs in different group, then that can be saved by tagging with different group.

1.3 Phone Book:

  • You can save Name and Phone Number of Person/Staff associated with any of your Company/ LLP/ Partnership Firms/ Proprietorship Firms.
  • Once you save name and number of any person and tag with any of your client, his details will auto fetch when updating records for In or Out of any DSC.

2.0 DSC:

  • Name of Client: Enter the name of person data of whose DSC is required to be maintained.
  • Company Name: Tag the name of Company/ LLP/ Partnership Firms/ Proprietorship Firms from the Master List.
  • Group Name: Details will be auto fetched as entered at the time of creation of Company/ LLP/ Partnership Firms/ Proprietorship Firms in Master Records.
  • Location: You can enter details where pen drive of DSC is kept like Token No./ Box No./ Drawer No.
  • Status: You can update the records In Or Out from this button.
  • Renewal Date: You can maintain the date of renewal/expiry of Digital Signature. This will help you to timely remind you for renewals and avoid last minute rush in peak periods.
  • Received By: Team member/ Employee of your organization can enter his/her name whenever he/she receives any DSC.
  • Received From: Name of the person from whom you have received DSC can be entered in this tab.
  • Note: Received By & Received From, both these fields will be enabled only when Status of DSC is updated to IN.
  • Delivered By: Team member/ Employee of your organization can enter his/her name whenever he/she hands over any DSC to client.
  • Delivered To: Name of the person to whom you have handed over DSC can be entered in this tab.
  • Note: Delivered By & Delivered To, both these fields will be enabled only when Status of DSC is updated to OUT.
  • Phone No: You can enter any WhatsApp number on which you want to send notifications.
  • If you have tagged any person in Phonebook with the client, the WhatsApp number will be auto-fetched which can be changed as may be required at the time of updating records.
  • DSC History: Entire log of In/Out of DSC with the name of the person in charge for receiving or handing over the DSC is available by just clicking on the name of the person.
